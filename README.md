Telco Customer Churn Risk Analysis & Actionable Insights Dashboard

Executive Summary: From Prediction to Actionable Financial Risk Management
This end-to-end Business Intelligence (BI) solution transforms customer churn prediction into an executive-level decision-making tool. It identifies customers most likely to leave, prioritizes them by potential revenue loss, and provides actionable retention strategies to maximize ROI.

âœ¨ Key Differentiator: Financial Impact Focus
While typical churn reports list "who might leave," this dashboard answers **"whose departure would cost us the most?"** It directs retention resources to customers with the highest potential financial impact, ensuring maximum Return on Investment (ROI).

Core Features:
*   **Financial Risk Score:** Churn probability multiplied by customer's annual revenue to quantify potential loss.
*   **Action Matrix:** Visual cross-tabulation of Customer Value (RFM) vs. Risk Level to isolate critical segments.
*   **Top 10 At-Risk List:** A ready intervention list for Sales/CRM teams, prioritized by financial risk.
*   **Visual Decision Aid:** Color-coded risk matrix for faster, data-driven decision-making.
